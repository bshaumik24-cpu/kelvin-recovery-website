Honest about how

Built for shared amenity spaces — and honest about how.

A cold plunge in a residential building runs unattended, in Gulf heat, used by residents you'll never meet. That demands commercial-grade filtration, chillers rated for high ambient temperatures, a documented hygiene program — and someone locally accountable for all of it.

Our systems are sourced through certified OEM manufacturing partners, then specified, installed, maintained and supported here in the UAE.

Process

How it works

  1. Site assessment. A free 30-minute visit. We confirm feasible locations, power and drainage, and give you a fixed quote.
  2. Agreement. Pilot, managed monthly, or purchase with a care plan — responsibilities clearly split in writing.
  3. Installation & commissioning. Typically one day on site. We test, balance and sign off before residents touch the water.
  4. Launch. Signage up, resident announcement out, usage guide live.
  5. Ongoing management. Scheduled hygiene and maintenance visits, all logged, with a monthly report to building management.

Questions

Frequently asked

Where are your systems made?

Sourced through certified OEM manufacturing partners, then specified, installed, maintained and supported locally in the UAE. We'll share the spec sheet and certifications for the exact model proposed for your building.

Who is responsible for maintenance?

We are, under every package — scheduled visits, water hygiene, filter changes and breakdown response, all logged and reported monthly.

What does the building provide?

An approved location, suitable power, water and drainage access (confirmed in the free assessment), and technician access. That's the list.

Is it safe for residents?

Posted usage guidance and health cautions, anti-slip matting, recommended adult-only access, a documented hygiene program, and an incident procedure aligned with your building's own.

Can we test it first?

Yes — that's exactly what the 60-day pilot is for. Fixed fee, fully managed, removed at no cost if you don't continue.

What happens if it breaks?

You call one number. Phone response the same day, on-site within our committed response time — and if a repair would take more than five business days, we swap in a replacement unit.
